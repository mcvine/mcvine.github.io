Dynamical structure factor

The sample kernel is defined by the dispersion $$ E(h,k,l) = 2.563 \sqrt{1-\gamma_{Q_{//}} ^2} $$ and dynamic structure factor $$ S(h,k,l) = \frac{1 - \gamma_{Q_{//}} } {2 \sqrt{ 1- \gamma^2_{Q_{//}} }} $$ where $\gamma_{Q_{//}} = cos(h\pi) cos(k\pi)$.
